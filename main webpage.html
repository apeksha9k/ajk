ABOUT US

AJK SWEETS & CHATS is a very popular Mithai & Farsan retail chain Headquartered in Thane city. It is synonymous to impeccable quality, taste and hygiene. With over 2 decades of expertise and experience,we offers a wide variety of sweets, savories and snacks that transcend the typical to traditional to innovative. Cultural research across Maharashtra, Bengal, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh and South India has added authentic tastes to a range of Mithai & Farsan that are peculiar of that region. We also offer tasty and wide variety of lipsmacking famous chats . At AJK, Chats are prepared and served in very hygienic conditions. The brainchild of Mrs. Aakansha J Karnik & Mr. Jay Karnik, the Mithai,Chats & Farsan retail chain is a benchmark for personal attention and customer satisfaction. The crowds that throng the stores on any given day and the beeline queue seen during festivals speak volumes about the relationships we have built over the years. Be it a festival, family celebration or corporate event, AJK Sweets & Chats is the first choice of the quality conscious.Visit your nearest store and get a first hand taste of the best in Mithai & Farsan. With the tremendous love showered on us by all Thanekars we have now 5 branches all over Thane.
